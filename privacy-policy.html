Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. What Data Do We Collect?

Short Answer: None from your invoices.

InvoiceBana is designed with privacy as the core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the data you enter into the invoice generator:

  • Business names or GSTIN numbers
  • Buyer details or addresses
  • Invoice numbers, dates, or amounts
  • Line items or product descriptions
  • Bank details or payment information

All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

2. What About Cookies?

We use the following cookies and tracking tools:

  • Google Analytics: To understand how users interact with the website (page views, device types, countries, etc.). This helps us improve the tool.
  • Google AdSense: To display relevant ads (after approval). AdSense cookies track general interests, not personal data.
  • LocalStorage: To save your cookie consent preference in your browser.

These cookies do not collect your invoice data or sensitive information. They only track how you use the website.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense: Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Formspree: For contact form submissions. Privacy Policy: https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/
  • Vercel: For hosting this website. Privacy Policy: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

When you submit a contact form, your name and email are sent to Formspree only (which handles contact form hosting).

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of cookies by adjusting your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

5. Security

InvoiceBana is built with security as a priority:

  • All processing happens client-side (in your browser)
  • No data transmission to servers means zero risk of data breaches
  • The website uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not store passwords or credentials

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Your continued use of InvoiceBana after changes means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, contact us at:

Email: support@invoicebana.in

💡 Key Takeaway: Your invoice data is 100% private. It never leaves your device. We only track anonymous website analytics to improve the tool for everyone.
