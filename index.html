Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool really free?

Yes, absolutely! InvoiceBana is 100% free and will remain free forever. We don't have any premium plans or hidden charges. Our mission is to empower every Indian small business with free invoicing tools.

Is my business data safe?

Yes. All your data is processed entirely in your browser. We never send your data to any server or store it anywhere. Once you close the browser, the data is gone. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I generate an invoice without GSTIN?

Yes, you can. GSTIN is optional in the form. If the buyer doesn't have a GSTIN, simply leave that field empty. Your invoice will still be valid and compliant.

What is the difference between CGST, SGST, and IGST?

CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) are applied when both seller and buyer are in the same state (intra-state transaction). IGST (Integrated GST) is applied when they are in different states (inter-state transaction). The tool automatically detects this based on the states you select.

Is a GST invoice legally valid if generated online?

Yes. As per GST Rule 46, an invoice generated online is legally valid as long as it contains all the mandatory fields (business name, GSTIN if applicable, invoice number, date, buyer details, item details, GST calculation, etc.). InvoiceBana creates invoices that comply with all these requirements.

What is HSN code and do I need it?

HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) is a code that classifies goods. It determines the GST rate applicable to a product. If your turnover exceeds ₹5 crore, HSN codes are mandatory on invoices. Use our HSN Finder tool to search for the correct code for your products.
