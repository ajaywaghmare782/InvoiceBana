What is a GST Invoice Generator?

A GST Invoice Generator is an online tool that helps business owners, freelancers, startups, and service providers create invoices that comply with the Goods and Services Tax (GST) laws in India. Under the Indian tax system, any business registered under GST must issue a tax invoice to its customers. A proper tax invoice must detail the value of supply, GST rate, CGST, SGST, IGST amounts, HSN/SAC code, and the GSTIN of both the seller and the buyer.

Using an online invoice generator simplifies the billing process. Instead of calculating tax rates manually or spending hours on Excel, you can use an invoice maker to automate calculations. For example, if you sell goods or services within your state (intra-state transaction), the GST bill generator automatically splits the tax into Central GST (CGST) and State GST (SGST). If the transaction is interstate, it calculates the Integrated GST (IGST).

For freelancers and small businesses in India, maintaining compliance can be challenging. A digital invoice ensures accuracy and prevents errors in GSTR-1 filings. InvoiceBana provides a free invoice generator that handles all of these tax calculations in real-time, right inside your web browser. You can select your state and the buyer's state, and the tool will instantly calculate the correct tax split.

By transitioning to digital invoicing, you not only ensure compliance with GST Rule 46 but also enhance your business's professional image. A clean, professional layout builds trust with clients, speeds up payments, and makes bookkeeping seamless. Whether you need a GST invoice generator for regular sales, a proforma invoice for advance billing, or a bill of supply for exempt goods, InvoiceBana is the complete solution. It is designed specifically as an invoice generator India to serve local business ecosystems, from small retail shops to large consulting agencies.

What is the difference between CGST, SGST, and IGST?

CGST (Central GST) and SGST (State GST) apply to intra-state transactions (seller and buyer in the same state). IGST (Integrated GST) applies to inter-state sales. Our tool detects this based on state fields and applies the split automatically.

Are digital invoices generated by InvoiceBana legally valid?

Yes. Under GST rules, any invoice that records required fields (GSTINs, business details, sequence numbers, dates, HSNs, rates, totals, and signatures/declarations) is legally binding. InvoiceBana provides all these blocks.

What is an HSN code and is it mandatory?

HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) is a product classification code. For businesses with turnover exceeding ₹5 crores, a 4 or 6-digit HSN code is mandatory on GST invoices. You can find codes using our HSN Finder tool.

Can I generate proforma invoices or bills of supply?

Yes. You can select "Proforma Invoice" or "Bill of Supply" from the "Invoice Type" dropdown. The tool adjusts titles on the generated preview and PDF output automatically.
